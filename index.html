  • https://takvimi.tk

    A Takvim is a calendar used by Muslims to keep track of important dates and events in the Islamic year. It includes information on daily prayer times, calculated based on the sun's position and the user's location. The prayer times help determine the appropriate times for the five daily prayers. Takvims may also include other important Islamic dates and events, such as Ramadan and Eid celebrations. It is an important tool for Muslims to stay connected to their faith and stay up-to-date with the Islamic calendar. (2023)
